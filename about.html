About Toonifyit

Empowering developers with efficient data formats for LLMs

Our Mission

At Toonifyit, we're dedicated to making LLM interactions more efficient and cost-effective. We believe that data format optimization shouldn't be complicated, which is why we created a simple, fast, and privacy-focused JSON to TOON converter.

What is TOON?

TOON (Token-Oriented Object Notation) is a data serialization format specifically designed for large language model prompts. It reduces token usage by 30-60% compared to traditional JSON while maintaining readability and structure.

Why We Built This

As developers working with LLMs like GPT-4 and Claude, we constantly faced the challenge of token limits and costs. Traditional JSON was eating up our token budgets, especially when dealing with large datasets or API responses.

We discovered TOON format and realized its potential, but there wasn't a simple, accessible tool to convert between JSON and TOON. That's why we built Toonifyit - to make this powerful optimization technique available to everyone.

Our Values

  • Privacy First: All conversions happen locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
  • Speed: Instant conversion with real-time output as you type.
  • Simplicity: Clean interface, no complicated setup, just paste and convert.
  • Open Source: We believe in transparency and community contribution.
  • Always Free: Core conversion functionality will always be free for everyone.
