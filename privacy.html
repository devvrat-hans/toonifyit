Effective Date: November 5, 2025 | Last Updated: November 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Toonifyit.com ("we," "our," "us," or "Toonifyit"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and share your information.

This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding personal data when you use our website and JSON to TOON converter tool at toonifyit.com (the "Service").

By accessing or using our Service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Converter Data: All JSON and TOON conversions are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any data you input into our converter tool. Your conversion data never leaves your device.

Contact Information: If you contact us via email or contact forms, we collect your name, email address, and message content to respond to your inquiry.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information through cookies, analytics tools, and server logs:

  • Device Information: Browser type and version, operating system, device type (desktop/mobile/tablet)
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, referral source, exit pages
  • Technical Data: IP address (anonymized when possible), browser language, screen resolution
  • Location Data: Approximate geographic location based on IP address (city/region level only)

2.3 Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your preferences:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Conversion history (last 5 conversions, stored locally only)
  • Delimiter and formatting options

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Maintain Service: Deliver the converter tool and website functionality
  • Improve User Experience: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance
  • Analytics: Understand how visitors interact with our site (via Google Analytics)
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads through Google AdSense and measure ad performance
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Communication: Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

4.1 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality (theme preferences, local storage)

Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics cookies to measure website traffic and user behavior

Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense cookies for personalized advertising and measurement

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

8. Data Retention
